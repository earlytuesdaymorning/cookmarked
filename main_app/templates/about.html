About COOKmarked


I have always had trouble keeping track of recipes I have used and enjoyed, especially since there just seems to be an infinite amount available in the age of the internet. Writing it down on paper is outdated and it can easily be lost. Besides, would you really want to have to write all that information down?


A great way to keep track of recipes you like is to simply bookmark the website in your browser! The trouble with that is that if you made any tweaks or adjustments to the recipe (like adding/removing ingredients, changing the amount, etc) you'll have to remember how you changed it next time you make it!


In comes COOKmarked! A simple-to-use way to keep track of recipes that you come across and create. Take it anywhere with it's mobile-friendly design! It also makes it incredibly easy to share with friends and family simply by sending a link! Don't like a recipe? Change or delete it with a few clicks! You can also look at a universal list of recipes to see what other users are making!


A simple solution for a minor problem, but it will hopefully reduce a lot of headaches for all the cooks and bakers out there!
